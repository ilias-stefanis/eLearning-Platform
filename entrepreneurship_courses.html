Entrepreneuership Courses

Expand your ability and readiness to develop, organise and run a business enterprise. Pick up valuable skills that will upgrade you as a person and as a leader. Learn how to create goals and achieve them.That is what entrepeneuership is about and our courses are here to do just that and more.

Our courses are focused on the mentality of a leader , an entrepreneur and mostly a critical thinker. 2.The importance of risk taking

Risks are part of our daily life, we take risks constantly with some being smaller and others bigger. Wether it's talking to a new group of friends or starting a business risks are important for our development and our propulsion to greater heights.Risks in business can turn out good or devastating.In our course we aim to teach new and experienced entrepreneurs how and when to take risks in order to minimize their potential losses and maximize the winnings wether that's a monetary gain or an achievement for your enterprise.

As a participant in this course, you will learn how to:

  • Create, identify, and evaluate new venture opportunities
  • The difference between gambling vs risk taking
  • The difficulties of entrepeneuership
  • Handling Responsibilities

Duration: 21 days , Price : 219€
